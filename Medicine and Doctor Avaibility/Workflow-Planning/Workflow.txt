WorkFlow Steps:

1>User Fills a Form with Speciality Doctor and Location Details
2>The Doctors are classified based on Speciality and Location
3>Doctor avaibility is checked through random forest applied on Doctor Dataset
4>Medicine avaibility is checked through random forest applied on Medicine Dataset
5>The results of step 3 and step 4 are stored in a third database
6>Random Forest is applied on the third database
7>The output is displayed with the prediction of Doctor and Medicine avaibility
